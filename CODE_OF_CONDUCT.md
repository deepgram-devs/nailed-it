# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1.

In short: be respectful and constructive. Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are
not tolerated. We want this to be a welcoming space for developers of every background and
experience level.

Report concerns to the maintainer at the contact listed in the repository (or, for Deepgram-hosted
copies, via the Deepgram community channels). Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject
contributions and comments that violate this code, and may ban contributors for behavior they
deem inappropriate.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant 2.1 is available at the link above.
