# Opener fragments

Lead the bit with fragments you've tested. Speak the **start** and trail off — the agent
finishes it. Open with a few of these to warm the room, then take fragments from the audience
once it's landing.

These are deliberately open-ended (they invite a surprising completion) and PG. Trim or swap
to taste; the prompt lives in [`config/agent.config.json`](config/agent.config.json).

- The best part of waking up is…
- My therapist told me to…
- The first rule of improv club is…
- I never travel without my…
- According to my smartwatch, I…
- The secret ingredient is always…
- In hindsight, naming the dog "Chaos" was…
- My password is so strong it…
- The Wi-Fi password is taped to…
- Honestly, the meeting could have been…
- My new year's resolution lasted exactly…
- The robots will rise the moment we…
- I told the barista my name was…
- Nothing says "professional" like…
- The fortune cookie said I would…

**Stage tip:** a slightly mis-heard fragment is often funnier than a clean one — the on-screen
text makes the mishear obvious. Lean in, laugh, move on.
